Our thesis

“The dignity of man is given into your hands; protect it. It sinks with you. With you it will rise.”

— Friedrich Schiller, after whom the Institute is named

Founded in 1984 by Helga Zepp-LaRouche, the Schiller Institute holds that beauty and truth are not decoration but the engine of human progress. Against a culture of pessimism and a system that treats people as costs, we organize for a renaissance — of classical art, of scientific discovery, and of an economics built on the creative powers of every person.
