The choice before us

They tell us the future is scarcity, conflict, and decline. For forty years, we have proven otherwise.

The Schiller Institute exists for a single proposition: that the destiny of humanity is creative, cooperative, and beautiful — and that a world of sovereign nations can choose development over war. It begins not with a policy, but with an idea about what a human being truly is.

Our namesake, our thesis

“It is through beauty that we arrive at freedom.”

— Friedrich Schiller, On the Aesthetic Education of Man

Founded in 1984 by Helga Zepp-LaRouche, the Schiller Institute holds that beauty and truth are not decoration but the engine of human progress. Against a culture of pessimism, we organize for a renaissance — of classical art, of scientific discovery, and of an economics built on the creative powers of every person.
