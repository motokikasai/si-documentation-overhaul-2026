We will not flatter you: the world you are inheriting is broken, and the people who broke it are not going to fix it. But you are not a consumer, a demographic, or a follower count. You are a member of the only creative species in the known universe — one generation deep, with the whole inventory of human genius behind you and everything still to build in front of you. This movement exists to train that creativity until it is dangerous to every dark age on offer. Not because it is easy. Because you were built for it.

No Vague Promises

Your first month, week by week

Movements are built from schedules, not vibes. Here is yours.

  1. I

    Read one impossible idea

    A single paper — how a physical economy actually grows — that will quietly ruin every economics headline for you, permanently.

    Get the first reading One evening
  2. II

    Join the international call

    Twenty countries of people your age, one video call, zero small talk. Argue, or just listen — both count.

    Get the call link One hour, weekly
  3. III

    Take the class — or the choir seat

    The economics class series, or a chorus rehearsal at the Verdi pitch. Ideally both; the geometry and the counterpoint train the same muscle.

    See the chorus page Two evenings
  4. IV

    Organize one person

    The hard one. Sit down with one friend and change their mind about what is possible. Everything in history begins exactly here.
