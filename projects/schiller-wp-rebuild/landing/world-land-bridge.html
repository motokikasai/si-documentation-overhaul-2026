The Record

Called impossible, then built

    The Productive Triangle

    As the Wall falls, the Institute proposes Paris–Berlin–Vienna development corridors to unite Europe — the seed of the whole program.

    The Eurasian Land-Bridge

    The “New Silk Road” blueprint: rail, water and power to develop all of Eurasia — published years before it became world policy.

    The world starts building
