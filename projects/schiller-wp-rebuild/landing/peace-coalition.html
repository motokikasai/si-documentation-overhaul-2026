The Framework

Ten principles for surviving the century

Proposed by Helga Zepp-LaRouche in November 2022 as the basis of a new international security and development architecture — debated since from Delhi to Detroit. Open each tablet; argue with it; bring your argument on Friday.

ISovereign partnership of all nations+

The new architecture must be a partnership of perfectly sovereign nation-states, resting on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the UN Charter — no blocs, no vassals.

IIThe end of poverty — everywhere+

The absolute priority is to alleviate poverty in every nation on the planet — achievable with technologies that already exist, the moment the political will exists.

IIIModern health care for every country+

A modern health system in every single country, to lengthen life and to make the next pandemic a managed event instead of a catastrophe.

IVUniversal education+

Access to universal education for every child and adult alive — because the creative powers of the individual mind are the real source of all wealth.

VA credit system for development+

Reorganize the international financial system so it issues productive credit for the real economy — the unrealized promise of Roosevelt’s Bretton Woods.

VIBuild the World Land-Bridge+

Modern industry, agriculture and continental infrastructure for every region — converging on one interconnected World Land-Bridge.

VIIThe end of geopolitics+

Respect the legitimate security interest of every nation, dissolve the logic of blocs, and ban the weapons capable of ending civilization.

VIIIThink as one humanity+

Real conflicts are resolved at a higher level than the quarrel — Nicholas of Cusa’s coincidentia oppositorum, the coincidence of opposites, applied to statecraft.

IXLawfulness of the universe+

Economy and politics must cohere with the lawfulness of the physical universe — the coherence expressed alike in science and in great classical art.

XMan is fundamentally good+

The assumption underneath the other nine: the human character is fundamentally good, infinitely perfectible — and every evil is a symptom of arrested development, which can be built away.
