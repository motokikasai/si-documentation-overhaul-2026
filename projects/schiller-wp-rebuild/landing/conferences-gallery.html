Archive treatment I of III  ·  The Gallery · The Firmament · The Programme

Schiller wrote that it is through beauty that man makes his way to freedom — so the Institute never treated its conferences as meetings. Each one was composed: the war panel resolving into the development panel, the physicist answering the poet, the whole two days closing on a chorus. What a museum does for paintings, this page does for those compositions. Nothing here is memorabilia. Every frame still argues, still performs, still recruits — and the paint, so to speak, is still wet.
