Contact · The International Schiller Institute

Write to us. A person answers.

Questions, invitations, disagreements, manuscripts, offers to help — the Institute runs on its correspondence, in six languages, and has since 1984.

An institute that holds open conferences must also hold an open door. Diplomats and students write to the same addresses below and are read by the same people; the question you are hesitating to send is usually the one we most want to receive. We do not sell your address, we do not answer with a machine, and we have never once minded a long letter.
